Phishing Game

This is the game I made at Northwell Health. When you lose, it displays a fact about Phishing attacks. The game is simple in which you use the arrow keys on your keyboard to move the fish around and avoid the emails with the anglerfish on it. M key is to mute the music and Space key is to pause the game. I used the Godot Engine to create this. Due to the limitations of the engine, the game plays best in Mozilla Firefox. Click on the image to play the game.

Phishing Game (TouchScreen)

This is the same as the above Phishing game, but it works on touch screen devices. Touch the arrows to move around, the sound icon to mute, and the pause button to pause. Click on the image to play the game.
